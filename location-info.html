Holland Park

Kyoto Garden

If the idea of a tranquil Japanese retreat tickles your fancy then try Kyoto Gardens in Holland Park.

The idyllic oasis was a gift from the Kyoto Chamber of Commerce, in recognition of the Japan Festival held in London in 1992.

It is exceptionally maintained with beautiful waterfalls, Bonsai trees and a pond (with a bright orange fish) which really does make you feel like you are away from the city.
